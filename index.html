Our Story

Surat's Trusted
Beauty Destination

Established in 2011, Grace & Glamour is Surat's premier luxury beauty and wellness centre. What started as a single boutique in Vesu has grown into one of the city's most-loved salon brands — with three thriving branches across Surat.

We are a leading beauty and wellness centre offering a wide range of services — hair styling, makeup, skincare, waxing, threading, nail services, and full bridal packages. Our highly trained team is dedicated to making every client feel extraordinary.
